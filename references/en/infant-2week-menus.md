# Infant 2-Week Menu Cases (by stage)

> Choose by age stage, then run 14-day rotation.

## A) 6-8 months (milk + 1 solids meal)

### Week 1
- D1: iron-fortified cereal + pumpkin puree
- D2: chicken-potato puree
- D3: carrot-beef puree
- D4: apple puree (small amount) + cereal
- D5: tofu-vegetable puree
- D6: salmon-potato puree (small amount)
- D7: combine tolerated foods

### Week 2
- D8-D14: rotate tolerated foods and gradually increase texture complexity

## B) 8-12 months (milk + 2 solids meals)

### Week 1
- Lunch rotation: tomato-beef porridge / tofu soft noodles / egg-yolk veggie porridge
- Dinner rotation: salmon-pumpkin mash / chicken-veggie porridge / potato-carrot mash

### Week 2
- Continue rotation, increase soft minced texture; log allergy/stool changes daily

## C) 12-24 months (3 meals + 2 snacks)

### Week 1
- Breakfast: egg custard + soft rice balls
- Lunch: minced-meat noodles + greens
- Dinner: shrimp-tofu rice bowl
- Snacks: yogurt / fruit

### Week 2
- Rotate proteins (fish/chicken/beef/tofu) and vegetable colors (green/orange/white)

## D) 24-36 months (3 meals + 2 snacks)

### Week 1
- Breakfast: whole-grain toast + egg + milk
- Lunch: chicken mixed-veg fried rice
- Dinner: steamed fish + vegetable noodles

### Week 2
- Keep meal structure stable; swap ingredients by family preference

## Execution rules
- Introduce one new food at a time; observe 2-3 days
- Avoid choking-risk foods (whole nuts, hard chunks, slippery rounds)
- Evaluate success by weekly trend, not single-day intake
