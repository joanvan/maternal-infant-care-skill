# Feeding & Sleep (Operational)

## 1) Is intake adequate? Use 4 markers
1. Alertness after feeds
2. Wet diapers (often >=6/day after day 5)
3. Weight trend (not single feed volume)
4. Feed tolerance (no persistent forceful vomiting)

## 2) Practical ranges
- Formula/mixed rough daily intake: ~120-150 ml/kg/day (individualized)
- Typical return to birth weight: around day 10-14
- If growth remains below expected trend for 2-4 weeks -> feeding review

## 3) Sleep duration rough ranges
- 0-3 months: ~14-17 h/day
- 4-11 months: ~12-16 h/day (with naps)
- 1-2 years: ~11-14 h/day
- Around 3 years: ~10-13 h/day

## 4) Stepwise bedtime routine
1. 20-30 min wind-down routine
2. Consistent bedtime window
3. Brief pause before intervention on night waking
4. Graduated soothing

## 5) Escalation thresholds
- Marked diaper drop + poor intake
- Repeated forceful vomiting
- Breathing abnormalities during sleep
