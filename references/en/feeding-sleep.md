# Feeding & Sleep (Operational)

## 1) Is intake adequate? Use 4 markers

1. Alertness after feeds (not persistently lethargic/fussy)
2. Wet diapers (often >=6/day after day 5)
3. Weight trend (not single-day fluctuations)
4. Feed tolerance (swallowing, no persistent forceful vomiting)

## 2) Practical intake ranges (formula/mixed feeding)

- Rough daily range: ~120-150 ml/kg/day (individualized)
- Prioritize total trend + diapers + growth over per-feed volume

## 3) Growth trend checkpoints

- Typical return to birth weight around day 10-14
- If growth remains below expected trend for 2-4 weeks, schedule feeding review

## 4) Sleep duration ranges (rough)

- 0-3 months: ~14-17 h/day
- 4-11 months: ~12-16 h/day (including naps)
- 1-2 years: ~11-14 h/day
- Around 3 years: ~10-13 h/day

## 5) Escalation triggers

- Marked diaper drop, poor feeding, dehydration signs
- Repeated forceful vomiting
- Sleep-disordered breathing signs (snoring/apnea/retractions)
