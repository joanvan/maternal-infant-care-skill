# Allergen Introduction in Solids (Operational)

## Rule set
- Introduce one new food at a time
- Observe for 2-3 days before adding another
- Prefer daytime first exposure

## Watch for
- Rash, vomiting, diarrhea, wheeze
- Severe signs: lip/facial swelling, breathing difficulty -> emergency
