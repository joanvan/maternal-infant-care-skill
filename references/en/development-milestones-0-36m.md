# Development Milestones 0-36 Months (Quick Guide)

## 0-6 months
- Head control, rolling trend, social smile, cooing

## 6-12 months
- Sitting/crawling/pull-to-stand, name response, imitation

## 12-24 months
- Walking progression, word expansion, simple commands

## 24-36 months
- Short sentences, pretend play, stronger social routines

## Red flags
- Loss of skills, persistent no-response patterns, major delay concerns
