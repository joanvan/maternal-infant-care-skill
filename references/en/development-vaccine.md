# Development & Vaccines (Operational)

## Monthly development review (4 domains)
1. Gross motor
2. Fine motor
3. Language/communication
4. Social interaction

If persistent plateau/regression for 1-2 months -> developmental evaluation.

## Vaccine visit checklist
- No acute severe illness at visit
- Bring vaccine record and prior reaction notes
- Observe 24-48h after shot (temp, behavior, local redness)

## Escalation thresholds
- Persistent high fever
- Poor responsiveness
- Seizure-like event or severe allergy signs
