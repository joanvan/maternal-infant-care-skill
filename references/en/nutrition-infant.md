# Infant & Toddler Nutrition (Operational)

## 0-6 months
- Milk-first approach (breast/formula/mixed)
- Track 3 markers daily: wet diapers, alertness, weight trend
- Wet diapers are often >=6/day after day 5

## 6-12 months
- Add solids gradually: one new food at a time
- Observe each new food for 2-3 days
- Start with iron-rich options first

## 12-36 months
- 3 meals + 2 snacks, fixed schedule
- Keep protein + vegetables in each major meal
- Limit sweet drinks/snacks

## Escalate care when
- Persistent poor intake + low diaper output
- Ongoing poor growth trend (2-4 weeks)
- Repeated vomiting, blood in stool, dehydration signs
