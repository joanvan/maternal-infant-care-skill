# Infant/Toddler Recipes by Age

## 6-8 months (puree stage)
- Iron-fortified cereal + pumpkin puree
- Chicken-potato puree

## 8-12 months (soft texture progression)
- Tomato-beef porridge
- Tofu-vegetable soft noodles

## 12-24 months
- 3 meals + 2 snacks + milk routine
- Example: egg custard, minced-meat noodles, shrimp-tofu rice

## 24-36 months
- Structured meals with protein + vegetables each meal

## Safety
- One new food at a time (observe 2-3 days)
- Avoid choking-risk foods (whole nuts, hard chunks)
