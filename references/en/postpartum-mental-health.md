# Postpartum Mental Health Triage

## Practical tiers
- Mild: short-term mood swings/fatigue
- Moderate: >2 weeks low mood, function impact
- Severe: hopelessness, safety concerns

## Home support actions
- Protected rest windows
- Explicit caregiving task split
- Daily 10-minute emotional check-in

## Escalate now
- Any self-harm or harm-to-baby thoughts -> emergency care
