# Sleep by Age (Stepwise)

## 0-3 months
- Safety first + day/night cues
- Short consistent bedtime routine

## 4-6 months
- Fixed bedtime window
- Pause 1-3 min before intervention at waking
- Graduated soothing

## 6-12 months
- Stabilize wake time first
- Keep naps age-appropriate to protect night sleep

## 1-3 years
- Predictable bedtime sequence
- Limit stimulating activities/screens before sleep

## Escalation thresholds
- Snoring/apnea signs
- Persistent severe sleep disruption >4 weeks
- Growth/behavior impact
