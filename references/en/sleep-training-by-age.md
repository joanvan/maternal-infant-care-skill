# Sleep by Age (Stepwise)

## 0-3 months
- Focus on safety + day/night cues
- Short consistent bedtime routine

## 4-6 months
- Fixed bedtime window
- Pause 1-3 min before intervention at night waking
- Graduated soothing

## 6-12 months
- Stabilize wake time first
- Keep naps age-appropriate to protect night sleep

## 1-3 years
- Predictable bedtime sequence
- Limit stimulating activities/screens before sleep

## Escalate
- Snoring/apnea signs, persistent severe sleep disruption, growth/behavior impact
