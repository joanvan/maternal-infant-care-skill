# Early Development Play (0-3 years)

## 0-6 months
- Eye contact, responsive talking, supervised tummy-time

## 6-12 months
- Naming objects, peekaboo, safe exploration

## 1-2 years
- Picture books, imitation games, simple two-step directions

## 2-3 years
- Pretend play, blocks, routine-based social learning

## Escalation cue
- Persistent social/communication disengagement or skill regression
