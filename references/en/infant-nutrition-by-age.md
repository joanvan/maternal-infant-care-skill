# Infant/Toddler Nutrition by Stage (0-3y)

## 0-6 months
- Milk-first feeding
- Track alertness, weight trend, wet diapers (often >=6/day after day 5)

## Starting solids (around 6 months)
Start when multiple readiness signs are present:
- better head/neck control
- interest in food
- improved swallowing coordination

## 6-8 months
- Milk + 1 solids meal, iron-first approach
- One new food at a time, observe 2-3 days

## 8-12 months
- Milk + 2 solids meals (progress to 2-3)
- Texture progression: puree -> minced/soft chunks

## 12-36 months
- 3 meals + 2 snacks
- Protein + vegetables in major meals
- avoid choking-risk foods

## Escalate
- persistent poor intake + low urine output
- poor growth trend for 2-4 weeks
- suspected allergy with breathing symptoms
