# Preconception & Pregnancy Playbook (Operational)

## 1) Intake checklist (required)
- Gestational week (or preconception)
- Age and prior obstetric history
- Chronic conditions (HTN/diabetes/thyroid/anemia)
- Current symptoms (bleeding, pain, headache/vision change, fever, fetal movement)
- Last prenatal visit + abnormal findings

## 2) Stage actions

### 1st trimester (1-12w)
- Establish prenatal care + baseline labs
- Small frequent meals + hydration for nausea
- Escalate for bleeding, severe pain, persistent vomiting/dehydration

### 2nd trimester (13-27w)
- Continue prenatal follow-up
- Track weight trend and BP if indicated
- Clinician-approved low-impact activity

### 3rd trimester (28w+)
- Daily fetal movement awareness
- Prepare birth bag/hospital plan
- Escalate for reduced movement, severe headache/vision symptoms, fluid leak/bleeding

## 3) Practical thresholds
- Most pregnancy concerns with red-flag symptoms -> urgent in-person care
- If symptoms are stable but persistent >24h -> same-day/next-day review
