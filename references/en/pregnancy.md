# Preconception & Pregnancy Playbook (Actionable)

## 1) Intake questions first

- Gestational week (or preconception)
- Age and prior obstetric history
- Chronic conditions (HTN, diabetes, thyroid, anemia)
- Current symptoms (bleeding, pain, headache/vision changes, fever, fetal movement)
- Last prenatal visit and abnormal findings

## 2) Stage-based actions

### First trimester (1-12 weeks)
- Set up prenatal care and baseline labs per OB
- Manage nausea with small frequent meals + hydration
- Escalate for bleeding, severe pain, persistent vomiting/dehydration

### Second trimester (13-27 weeks)
- Maintain routine prenatal follow-up
- Track weight trend and blood pressure if indicated
- Keep low-impact activity if clinician-approved

### Third trimester (28+ weeks)
- Daily fetal movement awareness
- Prepare labor bag and hospital plan
- Escalate for reduced movement, severe headache/vision change, bleeding/fluid leak
