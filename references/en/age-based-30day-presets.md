# Stage-based 30-Day Presets

## Pregnancy
- 1st trimester: nausea control + folate adherence + hydration
- 2nd trimester: balanced growth + movement + iron/calcium consistency
- 3rd trimester: fetal movement awareness + birth prep + sleep/anxiety support

## Infant/Toddler
- 0-3m: feeding stability + safe sleep
- 4-6m: circadian routine + developmental play
- 6-12m: solids progression + allergy logging
- 12-24m: meal routine + language/play expansion
- 24-36m: behavior boundaries + independent skills
