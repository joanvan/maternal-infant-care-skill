# Recheck & Follow-up Rules

## Home-observation tier
- Recheck key metrics every 2-6 hours initially
- Mandatory trend review at 24 hours

## Same-day/24h review tier
- Arrange in-person review same day or within 24 hours
- Escalate immediately if worsening

## High-risk modifiers
- Prematurity, chronic disease, immunocompromise, age <3 months with fever: lower threshold to escalate
