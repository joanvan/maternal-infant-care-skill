# Pregnancy Nutrition by Trimester (Operational)

## 1st trimester (1-12w)
- Goal: hydration, nausea-friendly intake, routine consistency
- Method: 3 meals + 2 snacks, small frequent portions

## 2nd trimester (13-27w)
- Goal: nutrient density, protein/iron/calcium consistency
- Method: protein anchor each meal, vegetables daily, iron-supportive meals

## 3rd trimester (28w+)
- Goal: symptom-friendly intake, lower sodium load, sustained energy
- Method: lighter evening meals, avoid late heavy eating, steady hydration

## Escalate
- persistent inability to eat/drink
- dehydration signs or rapidly worsening symptoms
