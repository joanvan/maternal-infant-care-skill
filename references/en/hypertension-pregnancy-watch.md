# Hypertension Watch in Pregnancy

## Daily monitoring
- BP at fixed times
- Symptoms: headache, vision changes, RUQ/upper abdominal pain, swelling, fetal movement change

## Home actions
- Sleep regularity, sodium moderation, medication adherence per clinician

## Escalate urgently
- Persistently high BP trend
- Severe headache/visual symptoms/chest discomfort
- Reduced fetal movement
