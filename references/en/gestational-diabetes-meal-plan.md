# Gestational Diabetes (GDM) 4-Week Execution Plan

> Educational framework only; follow clinician-specific targets and treatment.

## Daily rhythm
- Breakfast, 2-3 snacks, lunch, dinner at consistent times
- 10-20 min light walk after meals if approved
- Track glucose per care plan

## Week-by-week

### Week 1: Baseline tracking
- Log meals + glucose consistently

### Week 2: Carbohydrate quality
- Shift to lower-GI carbs and balanced portions

### Week 3: Protein/fiber optimization
- Protein anchor at each meal + 2 vegetable portions/day

### Week 4: Stabilize and personalize
- Build "safe foods" and "high-spike foods" lists

## Sample day
- Breakfast: whole-grain toast + egg + unsweetened soy milk
- Snack: small nuts serving
- Lunch: quinoa + steamed fish + broccoli
- Snack: unsweetened yogurt
- Dinner: tofu/beef + greens + small mixed grains

## Urgent review triggers
- Repeated out-of-range readings
- Worsening symptoms (dizziness, excessive thirst/urination)
- Any BP/fetal movement concern
