# Breastfeeding Deep Dive (Operational)

## Latch quality basics
- Deep latch (not nipple-only)
- No persistent severe pain after feeding

## Blocked ducts/mastitis signals
- Painful localized firmness, redness, fever
- Continue effective milk removal, supportive compress strategy
- Escalate if worsening systemic symptoms

## Pumping/storage basics
- Keep a consistent schedule
- Label milk with date/time
- Follow safe storage handling and avoid repeated reheating
